# AtomAI — 2-Minute Demo Script

## DSH Hacks V1

---

### [0:00–0:15] Opening Hook
**Visual: AtomAI dashboard — dark cyberpunk theme, animated atom on the left, AI chat on the right**

**Narration:**
"Materials science shapes everything around us — the chips in our phones, the batteries in our cars, the solar panels on our roofs. But learning it? That's been stuck in the textbook era. Until now. Welcome to AtomAI."

---

### [0:15–0:45] Feature 1: Atomic Visualization
**Visual: Type "Si" in the input box → electron orbits animate around a silicon nucleus. Switch to "Au" → gold atom with 6 electron shells spinning.**

**Narration:**
"Type any chemical symbol — from simple hydrogen to complex gold with 79 protons — and AtomAI instantly renders the atomic structure. Each electron shell animates at the correct relative radius, bringing quantum mechanics to life. No WebGL required — this runs on any device, even a classroom Chromebook."

**Action on screen:**
- Show Si → 14 protons, 4 valence electrons across 3 shells
- Click random button → element changes
- Point out the element info card: protons, neutrons, electrons

---

### [0:45–1:15] Feature 2: AI Professor
**Visual: Switch to the AI chat panel. Click "Explain band structure in semiconductors" → Professor responds with structured explanation including band gap classifications and Bloch's theorem.**

**Narration:**
"Meet Professor Aris Tensor — your personal materials physics tutor. Ask about band structure, and you get the full picture: valence bands, conduction bands, band gap classifications for metals, semiconductors, and insulators. Equations like Bloch's theorem are presented clearly — not hidden behind paywalls."

**Action on screen:**
- Show the suggestion chips
- Ask about band structure
- Ask about graphene → receive detailed properties and applications

---

### [1:15–1:45] Feature 3: Integrated Learning Flow
**Visual: Type "Au" in the chat → the atom viewer automatically switches to gold, and the professor explains its relativistic effects and plasmon resonance.**

**Narration:**
"The real magic happens when visualization and AI work together. Type a chemical symbol in chat, and the atom viewer instantly updates. Ask about gold's unique properties — the professor explains how relativistic effects contract the 6s orbital, giving gold its characteristic yellow color. This is learning that engages both visual and verbal cognition simultaneously."

**Action on screen:**
- Type "Fe" in chat → atom switches to iron → professor explains BCC/FCC phase transition

---

### [1:45–2:00] Closing
**Visual: Pull back to show the full dashboard. GPU simulation badge visible in the top bar.**

**Narration:**
"AtomAI is built for the future — with a GPU-ready architecture that can integrate real DFT and molecular dynamics simulations. It's materials science education, reimagined. Built with Next.js, TypeScript, and a passion for making the invisible visible. Thank you."
